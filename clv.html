// WHAT IS CLV?
DEFClosing Line Value = how your odds compare to the closing price
GOODYou got +105, line closed at -110 → +CLV → sharp
BADYou got -110, line closed at +105 → -CLV → square
// TARGETS
TARGET+1.5 to +3 CLV per bet average
SHARPConsistently positive CLV = edge exists
SQUAREConsistently negative CLV = losing long-term
// HOW TO CALCULATE
STEP1Record your bet odds at time of placement
STEP2Check closing odds at game time
STEP3CLV = (your implied %) - (closing implied %)
